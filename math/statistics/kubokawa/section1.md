### 問１

p2の対象差の定義と差集合の定義から
$$
\begin{aligned}
    A \Delta B & = (A - B) \cup ( B - A)\\\\
    & = (A \cap B ^ {c}) \cup (B \cap A ^ {c})\\\\
    & = (A ^ {c} - B ^ {c}) \cup (B ^ {c} - A ^ {c})\\\\
    & = A ^ {c} \Delta B ^ {c}
\end{aligned}
$$

また

$$
\begin{aligned}
    (A \cup B) - ( A \cap B) & = ( A \cup B) \cap ( A \cap B) ^ {c}\\\\
    & = ( A \cup B) \cap A ^ {c} \cup B ^ {c}
\end{aligned}
$$
